Creamy mushroom soup

Description

Mushroom soup prepared with cream is very popular as a delicious and satisfying dish. The intense mushroom aroma creates a perfect balance with the cream. This soup is a warm and nutritious soup, it warms the soul, especially in cold weather. With its soft texture and rich taste, you feel like you are in a luxury restaurant with each spoon. You should definitely try it. Also, it's quite easy to make this soup at home.

Ingredients:

  • 25g dried porcini (ceps)
  • 50g butter
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 garlic clove (sliced)
  • thyme (sprigs)
  • 400g mixed wild mushrooms
  • 850ml vegetable stock
  • 200ml sour cream
  • 4 slices white bread (about 100g, cubed)
  • chives (and truffle oil to serve)

Steps:

  1. Bring a kettle to the boil, then pour the water over the dried porcini just to cover. Heat half the butter in a saucepan, then gently sizzle the onion, garlic and thyme for 5 mins until softened and starting to brown. Drain the porcini, reserving the juice, then add to the onion with the mixed wild mushrooms. Leave to cook for 5 mins until they go limp.
  2. Pour over the stock and the reserved juices, bring to the boil, then simmer for 20 mins. Stir in sour cream, then simmer for a few mins more. Blitz the soup with a hand blender or liquidiser, pass through a fine sieve, then set aside.
  3. Heat the remaining butter in a frying pan, fry the bread cubes until golden, then drain on kitchen paper. To serve, heat the soup and froth up with a hand blender, if you like. Ladle the soup into bowls, scatter over the cro√ªtons and chives and drizzle with truffle oil.
